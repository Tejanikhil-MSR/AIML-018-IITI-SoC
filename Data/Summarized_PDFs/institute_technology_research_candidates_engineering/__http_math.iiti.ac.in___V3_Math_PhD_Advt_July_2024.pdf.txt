Academics
The Department of Mathematics, established in 2009, is an institute of national importance with a strong focus on research and teaching. The faculty members are well-qualified to conduct research in both pure and applied mathematics. For admission, candidates must apply through the academic website, providing minimum educational qualifications such as a degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or other relevant field with first division, along with valid GATE/NET qualification or equivalent. The deadline for form submission is February 02, 2024. The application procedure involves a written examination and interview in person. For more information, please visit the academic website.
