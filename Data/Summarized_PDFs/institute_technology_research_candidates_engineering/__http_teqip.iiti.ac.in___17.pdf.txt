The Technical Education Quality Improvement Ministry of Education conducted a three-day Faculty Development Short Term Course on "Technical Education Quality Improvement and Powder Metallurgy" from October 10, 2020. The course aimed to improve the performance of faculty members in education, research, and organizational development in the field of Powder Metallurgy. The topics covered included powder production, microstructure through powder metallurgy route, origin history, and powder metallurgy product development.
