Interview Dates and Academic Calendars for AY 2024-25

The Institute of Technology academic calendar outlines key events and activities for the 2024-25 academic year. The spring semester registration, orientation program, and course adjustment periods are scheduled from March to May 2025. The mid-semester examination is set for March 10, 2025, followed by a break only for Preparatory students from March 11 to 16, 2025. Classes will resume on March 17, 2025, after which the course syllabus will be available. The last day of teaching is scheduled for May 24, 2025, and the semester will end with an examination on May 10, 2025. Re-examination will take place from May 13 to 15, 2025, and the grade submission deadline is May 30, 2025. The calendar also includes important dates such as Best Award nomination submissions by March 31, 2025, and research thesis submissions by May 23, 2025, among others.
