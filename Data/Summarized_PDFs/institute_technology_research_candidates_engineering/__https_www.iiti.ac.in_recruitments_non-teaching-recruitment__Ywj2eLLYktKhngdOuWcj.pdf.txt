The Institute of Technology, ranked among the top 14 engineering institutions in the country by National Institute Framework (2023), has announced an advertisement for non-teaching positions on a short-term contract basis. The institute is seeking excellent candidates with academic records and relevant work experience for Junior Assistant and Attendant posts. The essential qualifications include a Bachelor's degree in commerce with computer knowledge and minimum 02 years of experience in Finance, Research, Development, or Material Management. The age limit ranges from 21 to 35 years on the day of application. The salary for these positions is ₹25,000 and ₹20,000 consolidated per month, respectively.
