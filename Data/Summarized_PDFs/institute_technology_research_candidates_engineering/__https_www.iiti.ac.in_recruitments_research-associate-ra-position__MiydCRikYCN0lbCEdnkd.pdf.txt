Interviews/Faculty-Openings/PhD-Opportunities
The Institute of Technology is seeking applications for a Research Associate position in the research project on Technological Innovation and Intellectual Property. The principal objective of the study is to evaluate different policies designed to sustainable environmental innovation, including water waste agriculture and climate change innovation for small and medium enterprises by the government. Eligible candidates must have a degree or Master's/PhD in Economics, Management, Civil Engineering, Agriculture, Water, Public Policy, or related fields with research, teaching, and design experience. The selected candidate will be appointed on a consolidated salary basis, as per the institute's salary structure, and may also be provided accommodation based on availability. The fellowship amount may be taken as basic for calculating Medical allowance. Please apply here; no TA/DA will be provided to the candidates for the interview.
