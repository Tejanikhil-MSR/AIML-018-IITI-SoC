## Others

ace Foundation, a non-profit organization, aims to foster innovation entrepreneurship among students and assist them in progressing from one stage of business development to another. They have developed an incubator infrastructure that includes office spaces, advanced IT central workshop facilities, and sophisticated instrumentation for fabrication. The foundation has received several awards, including the Best Start-up Initiative by Smart Economic Times in 2020 and the first prize at International Trade Fair in 2022. Additionally, they have a core group member participating in the Drone Challenge 2023.
