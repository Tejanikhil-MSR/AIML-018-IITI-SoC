Academics
The School of Social Institute of Technology is seeking applications from candidates with a four-year degree or equivalent in any subject, along with NET/GATE qualification. The institute offers research programs in Economics, History, Philosophy, Psychology, and Sociology. The application deadline for the TA category is May 18, 2025, while the non-TA category has a deadline of April 27, 2025. For more information, interested candidates can contact the Institute of Technology at 453-552 or visit their website.
