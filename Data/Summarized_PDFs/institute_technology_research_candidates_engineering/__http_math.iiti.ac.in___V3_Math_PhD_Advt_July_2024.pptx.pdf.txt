Academics
The Institute of Technology's Department of Mathematics invites applications for admission to its programs, with a minimum educational qualification and examination in mathematics or relevant field. The eligibility criteria include a degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or other relevant fields, with first division as defined by the institute. Additionally, valid GATE/NET qualifications are required. Applications must be submitted through the academic portal, which will generate a unique application number for each applicant. A non-refundable application fee is applicable, and candidates must arrange recommendations from at least two academics who have worked with them for at least one year. The deadline for form submission is not specified in the provided text, but applicants will be informed of the written examination and interview dates through the academic portal.
