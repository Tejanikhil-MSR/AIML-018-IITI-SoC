Academics
The Institute of Technology has announced an advertisement for Junior Research Fellow positions in the research project "Development of single phase refractory high entropy alloys for high temperature applications". The project aims to study the mechanical behavior, corrosion and oxidation properties of single phase refractory high entropy alloys at different temperatures. The selected candidates will have a Master's degree in Metallurgical Engineering or equivalent with first division as defined by the Institute University. NET/GATE qualified candidates will be given preference. The fellowship amount is 37,000 per month for the first two years and 42,000 per month for the third year. Interested candidates must send their detailed CV, statement of purpose (SOP), and educational certificates to the Principal Investigator by June 5th, 2025. Only shortlisted applicants will be informed about the date of interview.
