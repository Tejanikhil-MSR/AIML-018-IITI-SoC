Rules&Regulations/Policies
The Institute has introduced a Zero Tolerance Policy on ragging, making it a punishable offense according to the Honorable Supreme Court. The Anti Ragging and Anti Harassment Committee has been formed to take immediate action in case of any incident brought to their notice. The committee consists of the Convener (Chief Warden), Wardens from various halls, Academic Secretary, Student Member Gymkhana Executive, Chief Security Officer, and Members. Any student complaint regarding ragging or harassment can be dropped through mail to [institute email] or by dropping a letter at the designated boxes across the campus. Contact details of committee members are available on display boards and contact numbers are 0731-2438-717 (Emergency) and 1800-180-5522 (Toll-free).
