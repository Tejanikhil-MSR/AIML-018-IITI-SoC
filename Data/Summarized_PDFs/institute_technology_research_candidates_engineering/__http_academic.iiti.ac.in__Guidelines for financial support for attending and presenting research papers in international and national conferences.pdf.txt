Rules&Regulations/Policies
The Central Committee provides financial support to students under FA TA category for research purposes, with a maximum limit of 20,000. The Institute will consider providing financial assistance for travel grants to attend national and international conferences, with priority given to Research of year onwards. To be eligible, students must submit an application along with proof of acceptance from the conference organizer, and provide relevant supporting documents. The grant may be used for conference registration fee, air travel, insurance, and living expenses during the duration of the conference. Upon settlement of financial assistance, students must list all expenses in an orderly manner, providing supporting documents within 15 days of their return journey to the Finance office through their faculty advisor or supervisor.
