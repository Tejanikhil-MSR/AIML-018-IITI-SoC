The School of Social Institute of Technology is a vibrant research-oriented institution established by the Government of in 2009. It offers six disciplines: Economics, History, Philosophy, Psychology, and Sociology. The school has a strong focus on research, with various programs for admission to the Research program. The eligibility criteria include a Four-year degree or degree MA or Equivalent or Five year degree with first division and equivalent to international standards as assessed by the Institute in any subject and NET/GATE qualified.
