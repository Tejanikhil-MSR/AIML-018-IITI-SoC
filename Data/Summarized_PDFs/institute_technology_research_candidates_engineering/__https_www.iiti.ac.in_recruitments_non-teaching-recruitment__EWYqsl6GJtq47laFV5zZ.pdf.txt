Rules&Regulations/Policies
The 453 552 Institute of Technology has released Scheme of Examination for the post of Staff Nurse, Advertisement No. 03/2022, dated November 22, 2022. The examination consists of two parts: Language Comprehension and General Awareness, with a total mark of 100 out of 120. The written test is only and shall be taken into account for final selection or of individual candidate. Only those who score minimum cut off in the examination as may be fixed by the at its discretion will be eligible for further stages of selection.
