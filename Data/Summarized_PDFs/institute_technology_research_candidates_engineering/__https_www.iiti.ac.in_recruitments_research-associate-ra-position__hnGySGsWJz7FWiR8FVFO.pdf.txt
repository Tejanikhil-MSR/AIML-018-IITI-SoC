Academics
The LIT Institute of Technology Department of Mechanical Engineering invites applications from eligible candidates for a Research Scientist position in the research project titled "Design and development of functionally graded material direct metal laser for dental implant application". The eligibility criteria include having a Tech degree in Mechanical or Metallurgy Engineering with at least two years of research experience in relevant fields. Prior experience in Additive manufacturing will be desirable. The selected candidate will receive a stipend of 56,000 per month and the appointment is for six months. Interested candidates are required to submit their detailed CVs to the Department of Mechanical Engineering via mail by September 18, 2024. Only shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview via online mode, and no TA/DA will be provided for the interview. The appointment is purely temporary and terminable with the project.
