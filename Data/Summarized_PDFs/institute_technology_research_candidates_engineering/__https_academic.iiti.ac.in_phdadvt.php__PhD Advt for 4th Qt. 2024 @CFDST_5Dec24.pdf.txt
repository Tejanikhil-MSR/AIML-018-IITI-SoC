## Academics
The Institute of Technology is offering a program in Center of Futuristic Defense and Space Technology, seeking candidates with a minimum educational essential criterion degree with first division as defined by the Institute. Eligible applicants are strongly advised to visit the faculty webpage at academic.in before applying for the program. The eligible criteria include degrees in various engineering fields such as Mechanical Engineering, Machine System Design, Thermal Engineering, Heat Power Energy, and more. Last date of application is 24/12/14.
