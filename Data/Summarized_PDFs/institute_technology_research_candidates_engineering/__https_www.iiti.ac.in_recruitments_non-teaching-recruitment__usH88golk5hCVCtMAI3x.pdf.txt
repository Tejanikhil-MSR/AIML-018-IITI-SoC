Rules&Regulations/Policies
The Institute of Technology Recruitment Cell has announced the recruitment process for the post of Assistant Registrar against lien vacancy, with a deadline of November 1, 2023. The selection process will consist of a written examination and interview, to be held tentatively on January 23, 2023, and 2023 respectively. Candidates are required to apply with their application reference number and roll number for the post, with a total of five vacancies available. Note that call letters for the written examination and interview will be sent separately.
