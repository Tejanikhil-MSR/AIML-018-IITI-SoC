Club-Activities/Events/Conferences/Hackathons The Institute of Technology is hosting a Wellness Manager position as part of its on-campus activities. The role involves engaging with students, assisting them with physical and mental health, and providing encouragement to excel in their education. The essential qualifications include a Post Graduate degree in Psychiatric Social Work or Medical Science, with a consolidated salary range of 40-50 lakhs per annum. The selected candidate will be expected to maintain case records, prepare reports for each student, and attend to crises on campus at any time. The engagement is initially for one year, renewable annually up to a maximum duration of five years based on satisfactory performance and discretion of the institute. The last day for application is June 2025.
