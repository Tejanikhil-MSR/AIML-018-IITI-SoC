Academics The Department of Computer Science at Global University is proud to announce its 2015-2016 Fellowship Program, open to exceptional students from all over the world. The program aims to foster innovative research and collaboration among doctoral faculty members and their protégés. The fellowship is available in various fields, including computer science, engineering, security, cloud, electrical and mechanical engineering, physical chemistry, material and physics, mathematical analytics of massive scale data with uncertainty, public sector, business, urban policy, and analytics. Students can expect close interaction with mentors to develop innovative solutions for real-world problems. The program also emphasizes diversity and inclusivity, encouraging nominations from underrepresented groups. The application process requires a departmental endorsement, student resume, and recommendation letter. Interested students should submit their applications by [mid-2015] for the academic year 2015-2016.
