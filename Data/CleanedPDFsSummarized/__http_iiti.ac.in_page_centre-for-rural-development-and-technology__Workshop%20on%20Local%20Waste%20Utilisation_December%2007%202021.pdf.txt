**Club-Activities/Events/Conferences/Hackathons** The Institute of Technology for Rural Development and Technology, in association with the Department of Civil Engineering, is organizing a One Day Workshop on Local Waste in Rural Construction on March 24, 2021. The workshop aims to highlight the needs and potential utilization of locally available materials for sustainable construction in rural areas. The event will cover sessions on assessment and potential utilization of local waste for construction in rural areas, and is open to anyone interested in rural development, sustainable construction, or waste management. The last date of application is January 18, 2021, with confirmation of registration by January 20, 2021.
