Academics The Dissertation Policy at Institute of Technology from 2025 to 2025 offers a unique opportunity for students to undertake their dissertation work under the guidance of faculty members. The application process requires interested candidates to complete an application form, providing personal and preferred faculty mentor details. The deadline to apply is June 20, 2024, by 00:17:00. Each faculty member can select only one student, either undergraduate or postgraduate, for the dissertation work. Selected students will receive a payment link to pay the dissertation fee from the Office.
