Academics The Institute of Technology is seeking a Junior Research Fellow to work on the project "High Temperature Fretting Fatigue Behavior of Martensitic Steel" under project number 2023-007724. The essential qualifications include a degree in mechanical metallurgy production or relevant branches of Engineering Science, with GATE qualification preferred.
