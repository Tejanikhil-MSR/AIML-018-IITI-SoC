Rules&Regulations/Policies The Institute Technology Access and Policy outlines the rules of usage for students, including general do's and don'ts. The policy emphasizes respect for the system, accuracy, and completeness of accessed information, as well as copyright protection and reporting of any unusual occurrences. Additionally, the policy prohibits accessing or sharing unauthorized content, attempting to bypass security measures, and attempting to deceive about one's identity in electronic traffic. Failure to comply with these rules may result in disciplinary action, including warnings, network disconnections for a week, semester, or entire stay.
