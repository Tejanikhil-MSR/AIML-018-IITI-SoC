**Rules&Regulations/Policies** The Institute of Technology has announced its recruitment process for various non-teaching positions, including Junior Assistant, Lab Assistant, Superintendent, Senior Engineer, and Staff Nurse. The selection test dates are as follows: Junior Assistant on February 1, 2024, from 00 AM to 11 AM; Lab Assistant on January 3, 2024, at 02 PM; Superintendent on January 3, 2024, from 30 minutes past 12 PM to 3 PM; Senior Engineer on January 2, 2024, with no specific time mentioned; and Staff Nurse on April 1, 2024, from 10 AM to 12 Noon. The Scheme of the Examination and Syllabus are available on the institute's website under the "Career" tab. It is also mandatory for eligible candidates to make their own arrangements for food, transport, and stay as hostel and guest house facilities are not available inside the campus during this period.
