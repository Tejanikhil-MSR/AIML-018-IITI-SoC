Others JITI Foundation, a Not-for-Profit Company, is rolling out an advertisement for Consultant positions in Digital Twin Technology. The company, established as a Technology Innovation Hub for System Simulation Modeling and Visualization under the National Mission on Physical Sciences, aims to bridge the gap between technology innovation and societal needs. The consultant will work on projects such as Digital Twin in Smart Farming, Digital Twin in Blended Learning, and related fields. Eligibility criteria include a degree in Technology or Science from Tier I or II institutions, with experience in relevant fields. Compensation will be based on industry standards and performance during the interview. Only shortlisted applicants will be invited for an interview, and mere fulfillment of eligibility criteria does not guarantee selection. The position is on a contract basis initially for one year, which may be extended based on company requirements and candidate performance.
