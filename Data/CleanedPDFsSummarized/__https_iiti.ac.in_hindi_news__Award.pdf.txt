## Academics About 38 young scientists attended the Young Scientist Congress from March 17-19, 2023. The event featured a Young Scientist Program with disciplines including IT, and applicants had to meet specific condition for participation. The council provided an incentive to encourage students to participate by offering a Young Scientist Award in the IT category, which was won by a student who presented a paper titled "Hardware Accelerator Design High Level Compiler Driven Structural Transformation and Biometric for based Machine Learning" under their supervisor.
