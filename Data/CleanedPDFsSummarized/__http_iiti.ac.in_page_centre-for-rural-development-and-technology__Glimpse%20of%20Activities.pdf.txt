## Academics The Department of Physics at University of, in collaboration with the School of Basic Sciences, is hosting a short-term course on "Low Cost Housing for Rural Development" from June 2021. The course aims to explore various technological interventions that can be used to resolve simple and easily resolvable issues faced by rural communities. The session will be led by Associate Professor who has a strong background in Computational Condensed Matter Physics and has worked as an Assistant Professor at ae, Associate Professor at, and PI Uplifting Si.
