Academics The Institute of Technology offers a short-term course on uncertainty quantification, focusing on the framework of statistical approaches. The aim is to introduce the practical aspects and encourage participants to apply them in their teaching, research, or real-world projects. The course covers topics such as basic probability, regression, generalized linear regression, chain sampling, Metropolis algorithm, convergence assessment, and model predictive accuracy. Registration details include a waiver for students and faculty members, with a fee of ₹15,000 for industry personnel and ₹10,000 for service tax. Payment can be made through bank transfer or online link, and proof of payment must be sent to the instructor via email.
