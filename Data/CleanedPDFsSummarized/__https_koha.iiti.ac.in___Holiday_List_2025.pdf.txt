Rules&Regulations/Policies The Institute of Technology has issued a circular outlining the list of government and restricted holidays for the year 2025. The list includes Republic Day (26th January), Maha Shivaratri (26th February), Independence Day (15th August), Guru Birthday (20th September), among others. Individual faculty and staff may have two restricted holidays as per their choice, while all holidays listed are to be observed by the Institute. This circular is applicable from the year 2025 onwards, and utmost care has been taken to ensure its accuracy. In case of any discrepancy in interpretation, the English version shall prevail.
