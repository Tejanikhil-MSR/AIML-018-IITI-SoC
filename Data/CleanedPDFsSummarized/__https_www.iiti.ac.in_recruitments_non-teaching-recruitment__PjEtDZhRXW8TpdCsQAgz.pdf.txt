## Rules&Regulations/Policies The Institute of Technology has introduced a new policy for Junior Superintendents. The policy aims to assess job suitability and personality through a written test on April 24, 2023. Unreserved UR category candidates with no application reference number will be considered eligible for the post. A total of 28 candidates have been selected based on their roll numbers. Additionally, 4 candidates from the Economically Weaker Section category will also be considered. The Job Suitability Personality Test is scheduled to take place on April 25, 2023, from 9:30 am onwards as per the notice issued by the Registrar on March 19, 2023.
