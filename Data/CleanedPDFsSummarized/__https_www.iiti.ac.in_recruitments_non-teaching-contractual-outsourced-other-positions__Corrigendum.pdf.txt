Rules&Regulations/Policies Corrigendum to Job Qualification of Finance Manager. Reference is made to the job advertisement on Date, 26, 2024, No., Sec., 2024, 03, regarding the available position in Foundation Institute of Technology. Correction is to the previously stated qualification: Post-Graduation degree in Finance and relevant experience OR qualified Chartered Accountant and relevant experience. The revised qualification now includes Postgraduate degree in Finance or related field with relevant experience, CA or other relevant finance with relevant experience. This corrigendum is with the approval of the competent authority to revise the qualification for the position.
