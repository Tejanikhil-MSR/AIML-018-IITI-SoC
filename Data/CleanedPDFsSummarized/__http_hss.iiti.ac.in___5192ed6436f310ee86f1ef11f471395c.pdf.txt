**Club-Activities/Events/Conferences/Hackathons** The two-day workshop on "Contemporary Cinema" is scheduled to take place from 28th to 29th of September, 2020. The event aims to explore the ongoing global economic and cultural trends with their diverse expressions in new media, digital cinema, and multifarious cultural narratives. The workshop will engage participants in an intensive experience that delves into the aesthetics of cinema, both as a visual medium and a cultural product. The event is open to students, faculty members, and professionals from national and international backgrounds. Interested candidates can send a brief note and statement of interest not exceeding 500 words in either Word or PDF format by September 31st, 2020.
