Academics The Institute of Technology, No 48, A/A Road, 453-552, has notified the utilization of a research grant with contingency. The Board of Governors in its 43rd meeting approved the use of the research grant for various purposes, including support for travel, registration, accommodation, and DA for professional development and short-term fieldwork. Additionally, the grant can be used for international travel to present research work as a poster or oral presentation. However, candidates are not eligible for Institute support for within-abroad purposes. The contingency grant can also be used for testing and analysis of research-related equipment and materials. Furthermore, the grant allows for the purchase of non-consumable items related to research, including electronic goods, conference stationery, and journal subscriptions.
