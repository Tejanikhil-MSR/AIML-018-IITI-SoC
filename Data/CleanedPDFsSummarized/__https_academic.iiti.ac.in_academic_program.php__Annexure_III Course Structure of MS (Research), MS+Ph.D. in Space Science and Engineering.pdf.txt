Academics The Research dual degree in Space and Engineering at the institute requires a minimum educational qualification of a four-year bachelor's degree (BE) or two-year master's degree (ME) with first class or first division as decided by the institute. The program has a duration of on full-time basis with a maximum extension of one semester, and students must complete their thesis within the stipulated timeframe. For completion of the degree, students must have at least one journal publication in an indexed journal from their thesis.
