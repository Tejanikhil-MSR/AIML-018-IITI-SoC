Rules&Regulations/Policies The Income Certificate Issuing Authority varies across different states, union territories, and income certificate issuing authorities, including Tahsildar, District Magistrate, Collector, Revenue Circle, Circle Officer, Sub Divisional Tahsildar, Daman, Mamlatdar, CRO Tehsildar, Deputy Commissioner of Respective District, City Magistrate, and others. The authority responsible for issuing income certificates in each district includes the Tahsildar not below the rank of Tahsildar, Sub Divisional Officer, Tehsildar concerned, and others.
