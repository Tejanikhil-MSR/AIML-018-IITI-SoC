## Academics The Institute of Technology is inviting applications for a Postdoctoral Research Associate position in the research project "Technological Innovation and Intellectual Property" focused on sustainable environmental policies related to water, waste, agriculture, and climate change innovation. The principal objective is to evaluate different policy designs aimed at promoting technological innovation and ecosystem. Eligibility criteria include a degree or equivalent in research, teaching, and design with at least one research paper published in Science Citation Indexed journal in the field of Civil Agriculture Water Policy. The stipend for the Postdoctoral Fellow will be consolidated based on the salary structure of Research Associate Category (47,000-54,000) per month, decided by the selection committee based on qualification, experience, and performance during the interview. The initial appointment is till 31 March 2024, with a possible extension as per institute and agency rules. Interested candidates must submit their detailed CV to Prof via mail by 28 May 2023; no TA/DA will be provided for the interview.
