Academics Development of explicit solutions of near-surface waves in different strata of nonlocal elastic media with the Department of Mathematics is a research project that aims to focus on the mathematical development of near-surface waves, mainly partial differential equations. The essential qualifications for the Junior Research Fellow position include a master's degree or equivalent with at least 55% marks for general candidates and 50% marks for ST candidates, along with passing the NET GATE test. The age limit is relaxable up to five years in the case of belonging to Schedule Caste/Schedule Tribe and female, whereas non-creamy layer OBCs are eligible for a relaxation of three years. Interested candidates should apply through the LINK by submitting a soft copy of their application with all relevant documents in a single file, and the last date for submission is December 31, 2024.
