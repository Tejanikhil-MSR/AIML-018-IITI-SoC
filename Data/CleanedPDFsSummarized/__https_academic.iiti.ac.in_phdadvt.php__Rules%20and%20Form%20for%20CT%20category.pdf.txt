Rules&Regulations/Policies The College Institute University has established eligibility criteria and rules for faculty members seeking admission. To be eligible, the applicant's parent institution must be amongst the top 100 ranked colleges or universities in their respective category. The applicant must also be a permanent employee of the college or university and have at least one publication in an indexed journal or peer-reviewed international chapter book. Additionally, they should have a state-of-the-art patentable and transferable technology.
