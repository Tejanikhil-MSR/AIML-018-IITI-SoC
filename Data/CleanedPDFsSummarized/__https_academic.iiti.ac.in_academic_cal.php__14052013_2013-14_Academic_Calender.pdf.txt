Academics The Institute of Technology has published its Academic Calendar for the Year 2013-14, outlining key events and deadlines. The autumn semester begins on September 22, 2013, with classes starting for old and new students on September 23, 2013. The last date for registration is September 29, 2013, with a late fee applicable only for those who have taken prior permission from the competent authority. The mid-semester examination takes place from March 8 to 15, 2014, while the end semester examination occurs from May 23 to 30, 2014. Other important dates include the last date for submission of application forms (March 16, 2014), the last day of teaching (May 22, 2013), and the release of grades (May 21, 2014).
