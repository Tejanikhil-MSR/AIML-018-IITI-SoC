Rules&Regulations/Policies The Institute of Technology, an Institute of National Importance, has established certain rules and regulations for its visiting consultants. The engagement will be purely temporary and does not confer any right to claim the status of a regular employee of the institute. Visiting consultants are expected to abide by the institution's governing policies and safeguard the security and confidentiality of all official information. Additionally, there is no scope for negotiation regarding honorarium or other allowances. Termination of engagement will occur automatically upon completion of tenure or with one month's notice in writing from either side. The selection criteria include certificate verification and interview, with general experience being verified on the last date of application submission.
