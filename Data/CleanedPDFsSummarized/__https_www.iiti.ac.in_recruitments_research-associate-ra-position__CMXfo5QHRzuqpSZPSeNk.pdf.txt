Rules&Regulations/Policies The Institute of Technology has issued an advertisement for the recruitment of Research Associates (RAs) under the DAE project "Catalytic derived from single source molecular precursor". The eligibility criteria include a very good track record, excellent research skills, and good communication. Those who have completed their thesis but are yet to receive their degree are also eligible to apply. The selected RAs will be given a consolidated fellowship of Rs 47,000 per month for an initial period of one year. The application process involves sending the complete CV with address and cell phone number to Shaikh in by the last date of application (18th [month], 2022).
