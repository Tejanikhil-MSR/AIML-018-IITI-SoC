Rules&Regulations/Policies Form for No Objection cum Experience Certificate from the University College Institution for Applicant under College Teacher Category must be on letterhead of the Institute. The form should include a certification that the applicant is a permanent employee of the institution, their designation, and that they will continue to receive full salary during the duration of their program on full-time basis at the designated school center. The institute will also provide support for the same and relieve the applicant from all duties to enable them to complete the program within the specified duration or timeframe.
