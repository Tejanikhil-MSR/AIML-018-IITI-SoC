Academics The Institute of Technology Department of Mathematics is inviting applications from research scholars and students for the academic year 2025. The department offers research opportunities in areas such as Time Frequency Analysis, Harmonic Analysis, Mathematical Logic, Dynamical and Modeling, Machine Learning and Deep Learning, Numerical Linear Algebra, Complex Analysis, and Special Applied Mathematics. To apply, candidates must submit their application through the academic department, which will generate a unique application number for each applicant. The application fee is not specified, but candidates are required to arrange for recommendations from at least two academic supervisors who can vouch for their academic credentials. The application deadline is March 28, 2025, and a written exam and in-person interview will be conducted for shortlisted applicants. The minimum educational qualification is a degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or a relevant field with first division, along with a valid GATE qualification or NET category. For more information, candidates are advised to visit the academic department's website.
