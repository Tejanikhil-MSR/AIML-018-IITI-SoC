Academics The Institute of Technology has released its academic calendar for the year 2019-2020, outlining key events and deadlines. The autumn semester will commence on a certain date, followed by course adjustment and dropping registered courses. The last date to apply for conversion of Research Program to Dual Degree is September 15, while mid-semester examination takes place from March 24 to 26. After the break, classes resume on April 1. The last date for mid-semester evaluation of Project and Research Thesis is March 31. Other important dates include course registration by May 11 for the next semester, collection of answer sheets for End Semester Examination on May 30, and submission of report of project and stage of Research Thesis on June 19.
