Academics ``` The Institute of Technology has released its academic calendar for the 2020-21 year. The calendar outlines key events and deadlines, including the commencement of theory classes on March 20, 2020, mid-semester examination from April 14 to 15, 2021, end semester examination from May 15 to 22, 2021, re-examination for unforeseen medical emergencies only until May 22, 2021, and academic performance evaluation by the faculty members until May 31, 2021. The calendar also highlights important dates such as last date for submission of application form for remission of tuition fee on March 21, 2020, allocation of teaching assignments for next semester from March 21 to 28, 2021, and course registration for the next semester until May 24, 2021.
