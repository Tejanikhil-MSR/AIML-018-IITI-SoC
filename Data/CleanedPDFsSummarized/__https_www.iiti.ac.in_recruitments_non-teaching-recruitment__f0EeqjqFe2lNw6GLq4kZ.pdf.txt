Rules&Regulations/Policies The Scheme of Examination for the post of Junior Superintendent at Ra Institute of Technology Road 453 552 consists of written examination, with a total score out of 100. The examination will be objective type and does not include negative marking. It is divided into parts: Part Language Comprehension (20), Grammar Sentence correction One (120), Mathematics Numerical Ability (10), Arithmetic Standard Numerical Computation Numerical Reasoning Data Reasoning and Data Interpretation General Awareness and Current (10) and Domain knowledge (60). The written examination is only for the purpose of final selection or individual candidate, and only those who score minimum cut off in the examination will be eligible for interview.
