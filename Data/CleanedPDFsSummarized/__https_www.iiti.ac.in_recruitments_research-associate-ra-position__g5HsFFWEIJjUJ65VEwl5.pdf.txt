Interviews/Faculty-Openings/PhD-Opportunities The Institute of Technology is inviting applications for a Research Associate position in the research project on Technological Innovation and Intellectual Property. The principal objective of the study is to evaluate different policies designed to promote sustainable environmental innovation related to water, waste, agriculture, and climate change. Eligible candidates must have a postdoctoral degree or PhD with at least one research paper published in Science Citation Indexed journal in the field of Economics, Management, Civil, Agriculture, Water, Public Policy, or related fields. The selected candidate will receive a consolidated salary based on the following structure: Research Associate Category per month, and may be provided accommodation based on availability. The initial appointment is till 31 March 2024, and may be extended as per institute and agency rules. Please apply here by 30 September 2023. No TA/DA will be provided to the candidate for the interview.
