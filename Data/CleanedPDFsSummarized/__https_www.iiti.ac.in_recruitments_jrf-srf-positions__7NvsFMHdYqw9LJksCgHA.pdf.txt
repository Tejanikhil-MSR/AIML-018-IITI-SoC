Rules&Regulations/Policies The Institute has announced various research positions, including Field Assistant, Project Associate, Junior Research Fellow, and Research Associate. The principal objective of the study is technological innovation and ecosystem evaluation related to water, waste, agriculture, and climate change. Eligibility criteria vary for each position: Graduate degree for Field Assistant, Master's/Bachelor's degree for Project Associate/Junior Research Fellow, and a research degree for Research Associate. Stipends range from Rs. 18,000 to Rs. 67,000 per month. The initial appointment is till March 31, 2026, with the possibility of extension. Interested candidates must apply by October 10, 2025, without expecting any TA/DA for the interview. The selection committee's decision will be final, and the appointment will be subject to the Institute's approval.
