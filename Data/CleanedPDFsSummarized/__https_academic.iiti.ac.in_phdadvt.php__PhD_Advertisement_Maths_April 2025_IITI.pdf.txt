Academics The Institute of Technology Department of Mathematics is offering a range of research opportunities in topics such as Time Frequency Analysis, Harmonic Analysis, Mathematical Logic, Dynamical and Modeling, Machine Learning and Deep Learning, Numerical Linear Algebra, Complex Analysis, and Special Applied Mathematics. To apply, candidates must submit their application through the institute's website, which will generate a unique application number for each applicant. The application fee is available at the candidate's request. At least two academic referees with whom the candidate had an academic interaction for at least one year are required to send recommendations via the link provided. The minimum educational qualification is a degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science or other field relevant to Mathematical with first division as defined by the Institute/University and valid GATE qualification or NET Lectureship Assistant Professorship or equivalent fellowship. For more information, please visit the academic website.
