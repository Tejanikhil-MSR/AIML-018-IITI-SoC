## Others The Thakur-Sen Team from a village hospital and school visited on November 11, 2022, aimed at spreading awareness about cleanliness and containing the spread of diseases. The team comprising Thakur and Sen were made aware of the importance of hygiene culture amidst the current scenario and received information on various government initiatives for medical purposes. During their visit to the school, they interacted with staff and students on proper utilization of first aid kits, writing materials, sports equipment, and other essentials that were distributed by them.
