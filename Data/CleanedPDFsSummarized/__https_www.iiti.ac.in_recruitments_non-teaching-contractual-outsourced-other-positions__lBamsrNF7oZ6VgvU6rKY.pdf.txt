**Rules&Regulations/Policies** The Foundation Institute of Technology has established guidelines for data management and documentation, ensuring accuracy, consistency, and completeness. Key responsibilities include compiling and organizing data from various internal and external sources, maintaining confidentiality, and ensuring high standards of accuracy and integrity in documentation. Strong organizational and analytical skills are required, along with proficiency in Office, Excel, and Word. Ability to manage confidential data and maintain documentation integrity is essential. Prior experience in similar administrative or data support roles is desirable.
