**Rules&Regulations/Policies** The Institute of Technology has announced the written exam result for the post of Junior Superintendent. Candidates who have been selected are required to appear for the Skill Test, which will take place at the Hub Building. The above result sequence is not in order of merit, and no separate admit card will be sent out. Candidates are advised that entry will not be permitted after the scheduled time. Additionally, please note that changes to the date or time of the skill test will not be entertained. Provisional candidates must ensure that they meet all eligibility criteria as per the advertisement and submit original documents at the time of joining; otherwise, their candidature may be cancelled at any stage of recruitment without appeal.
