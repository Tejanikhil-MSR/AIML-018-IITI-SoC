Academics Admission to the Mechanical Engineering program at the Institute of Technology for AY 2025-26 is open. The application procedure can be completed through the academic portal, and a unique application number will be generated for each applicant. Eligible candidates include those with a first division degree in Mechanical Engineering or related fields, along with GATE qualification. International students are also eligible to apply, with no need to send hard copies of the application form and supporting documents. The deadline for application is May 25, 2025, at 23:59. For more information, visit the academic portal or contact the Mechanical Engineering Department office.
