Rules&Regulations/Policies Receipt Invoice for consultation, Investigation Ill, and Medicine Prescription must be accompanied by a prescription with the patient's name, age, and gender, along with the date, diagnosis, treatment advice, and signature of the consulting doctor with their registration number. The invoice must also include the name and cost of each test or investigation. Similarly, the Invoice for Dental treatment Answer Consultation Ill Prescription must be accompanied by a prescription with the patient's name, age, and gender, along with the date, diagnosis, treatment advice, and signature of the consulting doctor with their registration number.
