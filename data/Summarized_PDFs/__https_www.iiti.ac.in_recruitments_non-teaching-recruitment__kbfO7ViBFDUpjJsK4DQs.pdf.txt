Rules&Regulations/Policies
The Gar Institute of Technology has established a recruitment process for the post of Staff Nurse, with specific eligibility criteria and application instructions. To apply, candidates must have relevant experience in the field, with no restrictions on age or caste. However, those without experience certificates may submit representations with supporting documents by 30 IST on 21, 2023. The application reference number and position name must be included in the subject line.
