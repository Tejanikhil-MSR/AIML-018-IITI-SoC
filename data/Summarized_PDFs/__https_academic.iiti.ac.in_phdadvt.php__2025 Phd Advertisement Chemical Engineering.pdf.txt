Academics
The Department of Chemical Engineering at from is inviting applications for admission to its Program for Autumn semester 2025-2026. The program is open to students with a first division degree in Chemical Engineering, Material Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Technology, Polymer, Petroleum Petrochemical, Energy Science and Engineering, Civil Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Ceramic Engineering, Metallurgical Engineering, Mineral and Engineering, Rubber, Oil, Paint, Paper, Pulp, Textile, or Food Technology. Additionally, a valid GATE qualification is required. The application procedure involves applying through the academic portal with a unique application number generated for each applicant. The selection will be based on overall performance, which includes written exam, interview, and research aptitude. The deadline to apply is May 2025.
