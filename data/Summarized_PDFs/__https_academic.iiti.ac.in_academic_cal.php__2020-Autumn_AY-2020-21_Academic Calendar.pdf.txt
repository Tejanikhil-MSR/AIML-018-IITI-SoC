Academics
The Institute of Technology has released its academic calendar for the year 2020-2021, outlining important dates and events for students. The autumn semester registration program runs from September 17 to 18, with a deadline for registered course adjustments on September 24. Other key dates include October 10 for the submission of merit-cum-scholarship application forms, November 12 for mid-semester evaluations, and December 29 for the end-semester examination. Additionally, students can request re-examinations up until January 5, with practical classes and evaluation taking place from February to April.
