Academics
The Institute of Technology has announced admission opportunities for its Master's degree program in Chemistry, with a deadline of May 2025. Eligible candidates must have a minimum educational qualification of first division in their Bachelor's or Master's degree in Chemistry or other relevant fields, along with valid GATE/NET qualifications. Additionally, INSPIRE Fellowship and equivalent qualifications are also accepted. The selection process will be based on overall performance, academic background, suitability for research, and aptitude. Interested applicants must apply through the institute's academic portal by paying a fee of 8443. For more information, please refer to the main advertisement available at the institute's website or contact the admission office at Phone numbers 0731 660 3340 and 0731 660 3415.
