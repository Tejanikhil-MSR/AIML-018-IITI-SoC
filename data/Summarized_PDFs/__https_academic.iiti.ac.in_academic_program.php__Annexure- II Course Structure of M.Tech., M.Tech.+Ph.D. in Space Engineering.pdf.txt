Academics
Course Structure of Dual Degree Program in Space Engineering: The program is designed for students with a minimum educational qualification of four-year Bachelor's degree or five-year degree with first division, as defined by the Institute. The course structure consists of 71 credits, including core courses like Space Engineering Systems, Remote Sensing, and Satellite-Based Navigation. Students can also opt for elective courses from various departments, such as Astrophysical and Plasma Relativity and Cosmology, Computational in Astronomy and Space, Spatial Radio Astronomy, Galactic and Extragalactic Astronomy, among others.
