Rules&Regulations/Policies
The Institute of Technology has outlined rules and regulations for the engagement of a Sports Coach on a part-time basis. The appointment will be purely temporary and does not entitle any claim to regular employment. The Sports Coach must possess a Pass certificate, representation at the National All University level, and coaching experience in relevant sports. The engagement is fixed-term for 10 months, which may be extended based on performance review. Candidates are required to submit their application forms with all necessary documents by August 12, 2024, at 00:00 IST.
