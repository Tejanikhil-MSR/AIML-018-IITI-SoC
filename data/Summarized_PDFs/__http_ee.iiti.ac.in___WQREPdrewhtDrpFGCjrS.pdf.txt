Interviews/Faculty-Openings/PhD-Opportunities
Selected candidates will be involved in design, fabrication, electronic micro-fabrication, and optical characterization of on-chip devices. The initial appointment is for one year, which may be further based on performance. Selected candidates may also be considered for admission to a PhD program as regular full-time scholars. The amount of fellowship will be as per the eligibility and essential qualifications. Interested candidates must have a Tech degree in relevant branches of Electronics, Electronics and Communication Engineering, or Design with a minimum GATE score of 60 or equivalent in graduate and postgraduate studies. The age limit is 30 years, with relaxation for ST candidates as per Government of India rules.
