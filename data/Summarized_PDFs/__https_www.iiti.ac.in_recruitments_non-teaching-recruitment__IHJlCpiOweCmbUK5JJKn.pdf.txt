Rules&Regulations/Policies
The Institute of Technology has issued a notice regarding the recruitment process for the Deputy Registrar post. According to the notice, the shortlisting criteria include Master's degree with at least 55% or its equivalent experience as an Assistant Professor in a reputed institution, or comparable experience in research establishment and/or other higher education institutions. The upper age limit is 50 years, which may be relaxed by five years for those serving in Central or State Government Autonomous institutions. The notice also states that the gross salary of the candidate should not be less than the Basic Pay plus Dearness Allowance of the Pay Level in which they have experience.
