Rules&Regulations/Policies
The Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the eligibility criteria, step-by-step process, and required information for the Young Alumni Award 2025. According to the SOP, candidates must be 42 years old or younger as of June 30, 2025, and significant in one of the nomination categories: Academic Excellence, Public Service, Social Sector, Technology Innovation, Research Contribution, Corporate World, Entrepreneurial Excellence. The nominee must provide a Statement of Suitability, justifying their nomination with a compelling argument covering 250 words or more, highlighting the key achievements, recognition, impact on their field, society, or public at large, and alignment with core professional excellence, societal contribution, leadership, and innovation.
