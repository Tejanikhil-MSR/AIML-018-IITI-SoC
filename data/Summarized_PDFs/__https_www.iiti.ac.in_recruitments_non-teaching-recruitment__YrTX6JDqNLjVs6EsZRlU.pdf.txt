The Gwalior Institute of Technology has announced a recruitment drive for the post of Junior Technical Superintendent. The vacancy was advertised on February 23, 2023, and the application deadline is April 11, 2023. A total of 46 candidates have been selected for the written exam under the Unreserved category, while one candidate has been selected under the ST category. Note that calls have been sent to the above-mentioned candidates to appear for the written exam as per schedule. The date of the written examination is February 2, 2024, and it will take place at 4:00 PM at the Seminar Hall Complex or Lecture Hall Complex located on Road 453552, under the supervision of the Registrar.
