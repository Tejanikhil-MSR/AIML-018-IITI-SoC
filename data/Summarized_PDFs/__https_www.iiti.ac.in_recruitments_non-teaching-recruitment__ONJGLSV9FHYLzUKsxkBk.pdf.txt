The Department of Science Technology, Government of More, has established a Technology Innovation Hub for System Simulation Modeling and Visualization as part of the National Mission on Physical. This hub is being managed by the Foundation Section Not for Profit Company No Sec 2024 02 Date 22 2024, which aims to promote translational research at universities. The Postdoctoral Fellowship is designed for commercially focused doctoral research in physical and related domains. Eligible candidates must have a PhD from University Engineering Institute, with at least two publications in reputed journals. The fellowship duration is initially for one year, extendable up to two years based on performance and outcome. Applications can be submitted through the provided link bit.ly, and selection will be done through a peer review process.
