The Institute of Technology has announced the availability of a Research Associate position under an externally funded project. The Department of Mechanical Engineering is seeking highly motivated and eligible candidates to fill this role. The last date for submitting the soft copy of the application along with the required documents is August 18, 2023. The eligibility criteria include a degree in Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, or Instrumentation and Control Engineering, research experience in device fabrication and characterization after a postgraduate degree, and GATE qualification prior to the postgraduate degree. A track record of device fabrication and characterization will be given preference. The successful candidate will receive a remuneration of Rs. 47,000 as per the agency's guidelines. To apply, interested candidates must send their application, address, phone number, and relevant documents to the following address by August 18, 2023, with the subject "Recruitment of RA under project".
