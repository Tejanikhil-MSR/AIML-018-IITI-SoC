Rules&Regulations/Policies
Following are the guidelines for different types of leaves for faculty and institute members, effective from March 2020: Sabbatical Leave requires approval from HoD and Director; Maternity Leave, Child Care Leave, and Casual Leave require approval from HoD; All Other Leaves need approval from HoD or Dean; Research Academic Leave needs approval from Thesis Supervisor, Faculty Advisor, and DolA; Medical Leave requires approval from Senior Medical Officer and HoD. For extra-curricular activities, approval is needed from DoSA.
