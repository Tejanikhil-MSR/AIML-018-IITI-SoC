Academics
Research positions are available at the Institute of Technology. A Junior Research Fellow position is open to candidates with a Master's degree in Chemistry (65% or above) and a valid NET/GATE score. The stipend is 37,000 per month as per agency. The initial appointment will be for one year, and based on candidate performance, the tenure can be extended up to three years. Age limit is 28 years, with relaxation for ST and Physically handicapped candidates. Interested applicants should submit detailed CVs to Sarkar Department of Chemistry Institute of Technology via email with the subject "Application for position". The CV should include academic qualifications starting from 10th standard onwards, year of passing institute/university/college, work experience (if applicable), and nature of work. Incomplete applications will be rejected. The deadline for application submission is 2024.
