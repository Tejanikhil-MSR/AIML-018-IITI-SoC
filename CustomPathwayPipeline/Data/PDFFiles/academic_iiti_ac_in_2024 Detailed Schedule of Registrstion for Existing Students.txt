--- Summary for academic_iiti_ac_in_2024 Detailed Schedule of Registrstion for Existing Students.pdf ---

 IIT Indore has released a schedule for course registration during the 2025 Autumn Semester on July 23, 2025. Undergraduate students from various departments including CSE, EE, ME, CE, MEMS, SSE, ChE, EP, MC, and BSBE will attend designated lecture halls to confirm course registration and pay fees in sessions held both morning (10:30 AM - 12:30 PM) and afternoon (2:30 PM - 4:30 PM). Additionally, PhD students from Finance, CE, and MEMS, as well as BSBE students specializing in Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, HSS, and JPN will have separate sessions for their registration confirmation and fee deposit.
