 The Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IITI) uses the Cumulative Performance Index (CPI) grading system, where a score of 6.00 or above is considered First Class. The medium of instruction is English. A Deputy Registrar Academic Affairs contact detail is provided. There's an upcoming event on August 8, 2024, named Solan AAT Hela/IT/Acad./2024-25 at IITI.
