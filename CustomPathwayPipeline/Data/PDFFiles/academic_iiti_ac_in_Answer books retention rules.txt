--- Summary for academic_iiti_ac_in_Answer books retention rules.pdf ---

 The Institute retains corrected answer sheets from End Semester Examinations for one academic year. Access to these sheets beyond this period is not permitted. Before result declarations, the Institute makes available the corrected answer sheets of End Semester Examinations, Mid-Semester Examinations, and quizzes. Answer sheets of Mid-Semester Examinations and Quiz are returned to students following correction.
