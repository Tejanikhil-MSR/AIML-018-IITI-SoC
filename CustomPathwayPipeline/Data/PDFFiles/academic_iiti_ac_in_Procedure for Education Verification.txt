--- Summary for academic_iiti_ac_in_Procedure for Education Verification.pdf ---

 The text outlines the process for HR Companies/Agencies/Employers to request education verification from IIT Indore, located in Simrol, India. They need to send a written request via email or post, including student documents. Verification fees differ within and outside India, with different rates for email and courier services. Payment can be made through SBI Collect or using this link: https://forms.eduqfix.com/indoreiit/add. Contact details and address for the Academic Office at IIT Indore are provided.
