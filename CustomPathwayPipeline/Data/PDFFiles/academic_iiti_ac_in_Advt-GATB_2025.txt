 The Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IIT Indore) invites applications for its M.Sc. Biotechnology program funded by DBT, open to candidates with degrees in biology/biotechnology/agriculture. Admission is based solely on GAT-B scores. Minimum qualifications vary by category. The application deadline is June 22, 2025, and there's no fee. Required documents include academic marksheets, certificates, GAT-B Rank Card, identity proof, photo, and signature. A dual M.Sc. + Ph.D. program consideration is available for promising students after the third semester, subject to qualifying for certain fellowships. Contact information for queries is provided on the email addresses: pc-msc-bt@iiti.ac.in, hodbsbe@iiti.ac.in, and dpgcbsbe@iiti.ac.in. The program is offered in the Department of Biosciences and Biomedical Engineering.
